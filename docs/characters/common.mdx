# Common Characters ⭐⭐

Common characters serve as fodder and early-game helpers. While not endgame viable, they have their uses.

## Role of Common Characters

- **Early progression** - Help clear tutorial/early story
- **Fodder material** - Enhance higher rarity characters
- **Collection** - Completionists want them all
- **Budget challenges** - Some players enjoy low-rarity runs

## How to Obtain

- **Everywhere** - 74% of all pulls
- **Free summons** - Daily free pulls
- **Friendship points** - Very common

## What to Do With Commons

```
When you pull a Common:
├── First copy → Register in collection
├── Need fodder? → Use for enhancement
└── Inventory full? → Retire for resources
```

:::warning[Don't Invest]

Common characters are not worth leveling or gearing. Save your resources for Rare+ characters.
