# Characters Overview

Last Asylum features a diverse roster of survivors, each with unique abilities and roles. This guide will help you understand who to invest in.

## Character Rarities

| Rarity | Stars | Pull Rate | Power Level |
|--------|-------|-----------|-------------|
| **Legendary** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | 1% | Highest |
| **Epic** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | 5% | High |
| **Rare** | ⭐⭐⭐ | 20% | Medium |
| **Common** | ⭐⭐ | 74% | Low |

:::tip[Rarity Isn't Everything]

Some Rare and Epic characters outperform Legendaries in specific content. Focus on team synergy, not just stars!

:::

## Character Roles

### 🛡️ Tanks
Frontline defenders who absorb damage and protect the team.

**Key Stats:** HP, DEF, Resistance
**Best For:** Survival, protecting squishies

### ⚔️ Damage Dealers
Your main source of killing power.

**Subtypes:**
- **Single Target** - Boss killers
- **AoE** - Wave clearers
- **DoT** - Damage over time specialists

### 💚 Support
Keep your team alive and buffed.

**Subtypes:**
- **Healers** - Restore HP
- **Buffers** - Increase team stats
- **Shielders** - Prevent damage

### 🎯 Control
Disrupt enemies and control the battlefield.

**Key Abilities:** Stun, Freeze, Silence, Provoke

## Building Priority

### New Player Priority
1. **One strong DPS** - Your carry
2. **One healer** - Keep them alive
3. **One tank** - Absorb damage
4. **Utility** - Fill gaps as needed

### Investment Guide

```
Legendary Characters:
└── Invest fully if S or A tier
└── Check guides before investing if B tier or below

Epic Characters:
└── Many are excellent investments
└── Some outperform Legendaries

Rare Characters:  
└── Some are hidden gems
└── Great for early game
└── Useful for specific content
```
