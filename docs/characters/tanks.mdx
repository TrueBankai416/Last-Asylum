# Tank Characters 🛡️

Tanks are your frontline protectors. They absorb damage, taunt enemies, and keep your damage dealers alive.

## What Makes a Good Tank?

**Primary Stats:**
- High HP pool
- High DEF stat
- Resistance to debuffs

**Key Abilities:**
- **Taunt/Provoke** - Force enemies to attack them
- **Damage Reduction** - Team or self protection
- **Self-Sustain** - Healing or shields
- **Counter-Attack** - Punish attackers

## Tank Subtypes

### Aggro Tanks
Draw enemy fire with taunts and provokes.
- Best for: Protecting specific allies
- Weakness: Can't taunt everything

### Bruiser Tanks
Tanky but still deal solid damage.
- Best for: When you need tank + damage
- Weakness: Less protective abilities

### Pure Defensive
Maximum survivability, minimal damage.
- Best for: Stall compositions, hard content
- Weakness: Slow clear times

## Tank Gear Guide

**Main Stats Priority:**
1. HP% / DEF% on accessories
2. HP% on chest  
3. Speed on boots

**Substats Priority:**
- HP% > DEF% > Speed > Resistance

**Recommended Sets:**
- HP Set + DEF Set
- Counter Set (for bruisers)
- Speed Set (for turn 1 protection)
