# Support Characters 💚

Supports keep your team alive, buffed, and functioning at peak performance. A good support can make or break difficult content.

## What Makes a Good Support?

**Primary Stats:**
- High Speed (go first!)
- HP/DEF (survive to support)
- Effect Resistance (avoid disruption)

**Key Abilities:**
- Healing (raw or % based)
- Buffs (attack, defense, speed, etc.)
- Cleanse (remove debuffs)
- Shields (damage prevention)

## Support Subtypes

### Healers
Primary focus on restoring HP.
- **Best for:** Sustain fights, hard content
- **Weakness:** Can't heal through one-shots

### Buffers
Increase team stats and power.
- **Best for:** Speed comps, damage boost
- **Weakness:** Buff strip counters

### Shielders
Prevent damage before it happens.
- **Best for:** Protecting against burst
- **Weakness:** Shields can be stripped

### Utility
Cleanse, immunity, revive, etc.
- **Best for:** Counter specific threats
- **Weakness:** Often niche

## Support Gear Guide

**Main Stats Priority:**
1. Speed on boots (MANDATORY)
2. HP% on chest and ring
3. Effect Resistance if needed

**Substats Priority:**
- Speed > HP% > DEF% > Effect Resistance

**Recommended Sets:**
- Speed Set (most important!)
- HP Set or DEF Set
- Resist Set (for cleansers)

## Support Speed Tuning

Your support should typically move:
```
Turn Order:
1. 🛡️ Opener (buff/shield) 
2. ⚔️ DPS (attack while buffed)
3. 💚 Healer (clean up/sustain)
```

:::tip[Speed Matters Most]

A dead support heals nothing. Go fast enough to act before your team dies!
:::
