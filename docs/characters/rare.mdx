# Rare Characters ⭐⭐⭐

Don't overlook Rare characters! Many are excellent investments, especially for new players and specific game modes.

## Why Build Rare Characters?

- **Easy to max constellation** - Get copies quickly
- **Low resource cost** - Upgrade cheaply
- **Essential for faction content** - Some modes require them
- **Surprisingly strong** - Several rival higher rarities

## How to Obtain

- **All Banners** - 20% base rate
- **Friendship Summons** - Common drops
- **Free rewards** - Story, events, achievements
- **Connections** - Some farmable

Since Rares are easy to obtain:
1. Focus on utility over raw damage
2. Max their skills first (cheap!)
3. Use for guild content and towers
4. Keep copies for potential future use
