# Legendary Characters ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

The rarest and most powerful survivors in Last Asylum. These characters have the highest base stats and often game-changing abilities.

## How to Obtain

- **Character Banners** - 1% base rate, pity at 90 pulls
- **Limited Events** - Some Legendaries are time-limited
- **Selector Tickets** - Rare rewards from events

## Investment Priority

For Legendary characters, consider:

1. **Do you have the resources?** - They require premium materials
2. **Do they fit your team?** - Synergy matters more than rarity
3. **Are they limited?** - Limited units may not return
