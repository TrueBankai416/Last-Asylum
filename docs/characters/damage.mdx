# Damage Dealers ⚔️

Damage dealers are the core of your team's killing power. Without them, you can't clear content efficiently.

## What Makes a Good DPS?

**Primary Stats:**
- High ATK stat
- Critical Rate & Critical Damage
- Speed (for more turns)

**Key Abilities:**
- High damage multipliers
- Defense penetration/ignore
- Self-buffs and steroids

## DPS Subtypes

### Single Target (ST) DPS
Focused damage on one enemy.
- **Best for:** Boss fights, priority kills
- **Weakness:** Slow wave clear

### Area of Effect (AoE) DPS
Damage multiple/all enemies.
- **Best for:** Wave clear, farming, PvP cleave
- **Weakness:** Lower single-target damage

### Damage Over Time (DoT)
Stack burns, poisons, bleeds.
- **Best for:** High HP bosses, specific content
- **Weakness:** Slow, requires setup

### Nuke/Burst
Massive damage in one skill.
- **Best for:** One-shotting, PvP
- **Weakness:** Long cooldowns

## DPS Gear Guide

**Main Stats Priority:**
1. ATK% on weapon slot
2. Crit Damage% on necklace
3. Speed or ATK% on boots

**Substats Priority:**
- Crit Rate (until 85%+) > Crit Damage > ATK% > Speed

**Recommended Sets:**
- Attack Set + Crit Set
- Speed Set (for turn cycling)
- Rage Set (high crit damage)

## Building Your DPS

```
Checklist:
☐ 85%+ Crit Rate
☐ 250%+ Crit Damage (ideal)
☐ Enough Speed to take turns
☐ ATK high as possible after crit
☐ Skill-ups on main damage abilities
```
