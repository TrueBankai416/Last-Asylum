# Epic Characters ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Epic characters are the backbone of most teams. They're easier to obtain and upgrade than Legendaries while still being very powerful.

## Why Epic Characters Matter

- **Easier to max out** - More copies from pulls
- **Lower upgrade costs** - Save premium resources
- **Many are S-tier** - Don't underestimate them!
- **Essential for specific content** - Some bosses favor Epics

## How to Obtain

- **All Banners** - 5% base rate
- **Friendship Summons** - Some Epics available
- **Guild Shop** - Select Epics purchasable
- **Event Rewards** - Free copies during events
