# Boss Fight Strategies

Boss fights are the ultimate test of your team composition and gear. This guide covers general strategies and specific boss breakdowns.

## Boss Fight Basics

### What's Different About Bosses?
- **Massive HP pools** - Takes time to kill
- **Special mechanics** - Unique patterns to learn
- **Enrage timers** - Can't stall forever
- **Phase transitions** - Boss changes behavior

### Universal Tips

1. **Learn before you burn** - Watch the fight first
2. **Debuffs matter** - Defense break is huge
3. **Survival first** - Dead DPS does no damage
4. **Time your bursts** - Don't waste skills

## Team Building for Bosses

### The Ideal Boss Team
```
⚔️ Main DPS      → Your highest damage dealer
🎯 Def Breaker   → Increase damage by 30-70%
💪 Attack Buffer → Boost team damage
💚 Healer        → Sustain through fight
🛡️ Tank/Utility → Survive mechanics
```

### Key Roles Explained

**Defense Break (Essential)**
- Reduces boss DEF by 30-70%
- Multiplicative damage increase
- Keep it up 100% of the time

**Attack Buff**
- +50% attack to team
- Pair with def break for big damage

**Sustain**
- Boss fights are marathons
- Healing, shields, or mitigation

## General Boss Mechanics

### Adds/Minions
Many bosses summon helpers:
- Kill priority depends on mechanics
- Some buff the boss
- Some heal the boss
- Some just deal damage

### Enrage
If you take too long:
- Boss stats increase dramatically
- Usually a DPS check
- Need better damage or gear

### Phase Transitions
Boss changes at certain HP thresholds:
- New mechanics unlock
- Old mechanics may stop
- Sometimes healing phases

## Damage Calculation Example

```
Without Def Break:
Final Damage = 10,000 ATK vs 3,000 DEF = ~7,000 damage

With 70% Def Break:
Final Damage = 10,000 ATK vs 900 DEF = ~9,100 damage

That's a 30% damage increase!
```

## Progression Path

1. **Can't clear?** → Check your team comp
2. **Dying too fast?** → More sustain needed
3. **Running out of time?** → More damage needed
4. **Random deaths?** → Learn the mechanic
