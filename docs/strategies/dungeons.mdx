# Dungeon Strategy Guide

Dungeons are your primary source of resources, gear, and materials. Learn to farm them efficiently!

## Dungeon Overview

### Types of Dungeons

| Dungeon | Drops | When to Farm |
|---------|-------|--------------|
| Gold Dungeon | Currency | When upgrading |
| EXP Dungeon | Character EXP | Leveling new units |
| Gear Dungeon | Equipment | Always needed |
| Skill Dungeon | Skill materials | Upgrading skills |
| Awakening Dungeon | Awakening mats | Awakening units |

### Dungeon Schedule
Many dungeons are only available on certain days:

| Day | Available Dungeons |
|-----|-------------------|
| Monday | EXP, Element A |
| Tuesday | Gold, Element B |
| Wednesday | Skill Materials |
| Thursday | Gold, Element C |
| Friday | Gear |
| Saturday | All open |
| Sunday | All open |

## Building Dungeon Teams

### Speed Teams
For farming, speed is king:
- Faster clears = more runs
- AoE damage preferred
- Minimal sustain needed

### Safe Teams
For new dungeons or progression:
- Consistent clears > fast clears
- Include healer/sustain
- Focus on not failing

### One-Shot Teams
Best for high-end farming:
- Kill waves before they act
- Requires good gear
- Maximum efficiency

## Dungeon Strategies

### Gear Dungeon

**Element Advantage:**
- Bring the counter element
- 15% damage bonus + enemy takes more

**Team Example:**
```
⚔️ AoE DPS (counter element)
⚔️ AoE DPS (counter element)
💪 Attack buffer
🎯 Defense breaker
🛡️ Tank or extra DPS
```

### Gold Dungeon

- Usually simpler mechanics
- Pure AoE damage works
- Stack damage boosters

### EXP Dungeon

- Bring characters you want to level
- Fill with AoE damage dealers
- Friend supports help early

## Efficiency Tips

### Clear Time Goals
```
🏆 Optimal: < 1 minute
✅ Good: 1-2 minutes  
⚠️ Okay: 2-3 minutes
❌ Slow: > 3 minutes (improve team)
```

### When to Move Up Difficulty

- Can you clear reliably (95%+ success)?
- Is clear time reasonable?
- Do you have the stamina efficiency?

### Stamina Math
Higher difficulty = better drops per stamina:
```
Difficulty 10: 10 stamina for ~5 items
Difficulty 15: 15 stamina for ~9 items

15 gets ~80% more items for 50% more stamina!
```

## Dungeon Checklist

- [ ] Are you farming the highest reliable difficulty?
- [ ] Is your team element-optimized?
- [ ] Are clear times reasonable?
- [ ] Are you using all daily dungeon entries?
