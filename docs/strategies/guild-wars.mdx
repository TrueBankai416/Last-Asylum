# Guild Wars Strategy Guide

Guild Wars is a team effort. Coordination and strategy matter as much as individual power!

## Guild Wars Basics

### Format
- Guild vs Guild battles
- Each member sets defense
- Attack enemy guild members
- Points determine winner

### Seasons/Schedule
- Typically weekly or bi-weekly
- Matchmaking based on guild rank
- Rewards scale with performance

## Building for Guild Wars

### Defense Setup

**Your defense represents your guild:**
- Build your strongest team
- Consider tower/fort buffs
- Test against guildmates

**Defense Types:**
```
🏰 Fort Defense: Your main team, hardest to beat
🗼 Tower Defense: Secondary teams, protect forts  
```

### Offense Teams

**Build multiple teams:**
- Main offense team
- Counter-pick teams
- Cleanup teams

## Strategy Tips

### Communication is Key

:::tip[Guild Communication]

- Share enemy defense screenshots
- Call your targets
- Report results
- Coordinate attacks

:::

### Target Priority

**Attack Order:**
1. **Weak defenses** you can guarantee
2. **Key targets** your counters beat
3. **Support attacks** to help others

### Scouting

Before attacking:
- Check enemy team composition
- Look for weaknesses
- Select appropriate counter

## Guild War Phases

### Preparation Phase
- Set your defense
- Scout enemy guild
- Plan target assignments

### Battle Phase
- Execute attacks
- Communicate results
- Adjust strategy as needed

### Post-Battle
- Review what worked
- Identify improvements
- Celebrate or analyze

## Individual Contribution

### How to Help Your Guild

**Good Contribution:**
- ✅ Strong defense that wins
- ✅ Successful attacks
- ✅ Communication
- ✅ Flexibility

**Poor Contribution:**
- ❌ Weak/neglected defense
- ❌ Wasted attacks
- ❌ Going silent
- ❌ Solo mentality

## Rewards

| Guild Rank | Rewards |
|------------|---------|
| Losing | Participation rewards |
| Winning | Victory bonus |
| High Season Rank | Premium rewards |
| MVP Performance | Extra bonuses |

## Advanced Tactics

### Tower Strategy
- Clear towers to weaken forts
- Some buffs are more valuable
- Coordinate tower attacks

### Defense Baiting
- Set defense that looks weak
- Actually counters common attacks
- Waste enemy attempts

### Last-Minute Attacks
- Save attempts for crucial moments
- Coordinate final push
- Win close matches

## Guild Management

### Building a Strong Guild

**Recruit:**
- Active players over strong players
- Good communication skills
- Team-first attitude

**Maintain:**
- Set activity requirements
- Provide resources/guidance
- Remove inactive members
