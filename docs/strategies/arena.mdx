# Arena Strategy Guide

Arena is where you test your team against other players. Here's how to climb the rankings!

## Arena Basics

### Arena Format
- Attack other players' defense teams
- Win to gain points/rankings
- Get attacked and lose points
- Weekly/Daily rewards based on rank

### Offense vs Defense

**Offense:** You control the fight
- Pick favorable matchups
- Use specific counters
- Manual control if needed

**Defense:** AI controls your team
- Must work on auto
- Deter attacks
- Can't rely on complex strategies

## Building Arena Teams

### Offense Team

**Speed Cleave:**
```
⚡ Speed lead/buffer → Go first
⚔️ AoE DPS → Kill them
⚔️ AoE DPS → Clean up
🎯 Stripper → Remove immunity
🛡️ Backup → Insurance
```

**Bruiser/Control:**
```
🛡️ Tank → Survive first hits
⚔️ Bruiser DPS → Damage + sustain
💚 Healer → Outlast them
🎯 Control → Disable threats
🎯 Flex → Counter pick
```

### Defense Team

**Deterrent Defense:**
- High threat characters
- Fast/unpredictable
- Tanky + annoying

**Stall Defense:**
- Hard to kill quickly
- Heals and revives
- Makes enemies skip you

## Arena Strategies

### Choosing Targets

✅ **Attack When:**
- You have element advantage
- You can outspeed them
- You have direct counters
- Win rate estimate > 80%

❌ **Skip When:**
- Unknown units/threat
- Hard counter to your team
- Much higher power level

### Speed Tuning

Your team needs to move in the right order:
```
1. Buffer/Stripper → Setup
2. Defense Break → Amplify
3. DPS → Kill
4. Cleanup → Finish
```

:::warning[Speed Gaps]

If enemies cut between your units, you lose!
Keep speed gaps tight (within 10-15 speed).

:::
