# Campaign Strategy Guide

The campaign is your main progression path. Here's how to clear it efficiently.

## Campaign Overview

The campaign consists of multiple chapters:
- Story stages with increasing difficulty
- Boss stages at chapter ends
- Hard mode unlocks after clearing normal

## General Strategy

### Team Composition

**Standard Campaign Team:**
```
🛡️ Tank - Survive hits
⚔️ DPS - Kill enemies  
⚔️ DPS - Extra damage
💚 Healer - Keep alive
🎯 Flex - Utility/more damage
```

### Progression Checklist

Before moving to the next stage:
- [ ] Can you 3-star the current stage?
- [ ] Is auto-battle successful?
- [ ] Are your characters appropriately leveled?

### Early Game (Chapters 1-5)
- Focus on leveling your starter team
- Use friend support for difficult stages
- Don't worry about optimal compositions

### Mid Game (Chapters 6-10)
- Start building specialized teams
- Farm gear from dungeons
- Upgrade skills on main characters

### Late Game (Chapters 11+)
- Requires well-geared teams
- Learn enemy patterns
- Consider element advantages

## Boss Stage Tips

Every few stages features a boss fight:

1. **Learn the pattern** - Watch what the boss does
2. **Counter the mechanics** - Bring appropriate units
3. **Bring sustain** - Fights last longer than normal
4. **Use damage windows** - Don't just spam attacks

## Stuck? Try These

:::tip[Unstuck Guide]

1. **Level up** - Sometimes you just need more stats
2. **Upgrade gear** - Check your equipment
3. **Try different comp** - Maybe your team is wrong
4. **Use friend support** - Borrow a strong unit
5. **Come back later** - Farm and return stronger

:::
