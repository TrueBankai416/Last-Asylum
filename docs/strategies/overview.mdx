## General Principles

### Resource Efficiency
Every energy point should be optimized:
- Farm highest difficulty you can clear reliably
- Prioritize limited-time content
- Don't waste resources on unnecessary retries

### Team Flexibility
Build multiple teams for different content:
- PvE general team
- Boss killer team  
- PvP offense
- PvP defense
- Dungeon speed team

### Patience Wins
- Don't rush content you're not ready for
- Daily progress adds up
- Gear RNG takes time
