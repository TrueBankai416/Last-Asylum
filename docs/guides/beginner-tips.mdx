# Beginner Tips & Tricks

Starting a new game can be overwhelming. Here are the most important tips to help you progress efficiently in Last Asylum.

## Resource Management

### Don't Waste Premium Currency

Your premium currency (Gems/Crystals) is precious:
- ✅ Save for limited banners with guaranteed pulls
- ✅ Buy extra energy during events
- ❌ Don't use for speeding up timers
- ❌ Avoid regular banner pulls

### Daily Tasks Are Essential

Complete your daily tasks every day to maximize:
- Free premium currency
- Stamina/Energy refills
- Experience points
- Essential upgrade materials

## Character Progression

### Focus on Core Team

Instead of leveling everyone equally:

```
🌟 Main Team (5-6 characters) → Max investment
⭐ Secondary Team → Moderate investment  
💫 Others → Save for later
```

### Upgrade Priority

1. **Level** - Raises base stats
2. **Skills** - Increases damage/healing output
3. **Gear** - Significant stat boosts
4. **Awakening** - Late-game power spike

## Combat Tips

### Understand the Triangle

Most games have an element or class advantage system:

:::info[Combat Triangle]

- **Strength** beats **Agility**
- **Agility** beats **Intelligence**  
- **Intelligence** beats **Strength**

:::

### Auto-Battle Wisely

- Use auto for farming easy content
- Manual control for difficult stages
- Learn skill timing for boss fights

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | Why It's Bad | What to Do Instead |
|---------|--------------|-------------------|
| Spreading resources thin | Weak overall team | Focus on 5-6 main heroes |
| Ignoring events | Miss limited rewards | Prioritize event content |
| Skipping dailies | Slow progression | Do them every day |
| Rushing | Burnout & mistakes | Pace yourself |

## Quick Checklist

- [ ] Complete daily missions
- [ ] Spend all stamina before bed
- [ ] Check shop for free items
- [ ] Participate in active events
- [ ] Claim all mail rewards
- [ ] Do your arena attempts
