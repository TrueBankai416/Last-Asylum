# Game Mechanics

Understanding the core mechanics will help you make better decisions and progress faster.

## Combat System

### Turn Order

Combat in Last Asylum uses a speed-based turn system:
- Characters with higher **Speed** act first
- Some skills can manipulate turn order
- Boss phases may interrupt normal turn order

### Damage Calculation

```
Final Damage = (Base ATK × Skill Multiplier × Crit Modifier) - Enemy DEF
```

Key factors:
- **Attack (ATK)** - Base damage stat
- **Skill Multiplier** - Each skill has different power
- **Critical Rate** - Chance to deal bonus damage
- **Critical Damage** - Multiplier when critting (usually 150-200%)
- **Defense (DEF)** - Reduces incoming damage

### Status Effects

| Effect | Duration | Description |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| Burn | 2-3 turns | Damage over time (ignores DEF) |
| Freeze | 1-2 turns | Skip turn, take extra damage |
| Poison | 3 turns | % HP damage each turn |
| Stun | 1 turn | Cannot act |
| Buff Block | 2 turns | Cannot receive buffs |

## Character Stats

### Primary Stats

- **HP** - Health points, survive more damage
- **ATK** - Attack power for damage dealers
- **DEF** - Reduces damage taken
- **SPD** - Determines turn order

### Secondary Stats

- **Crit Rate** - Chance to critical hit
- **Crit Damage** - Bonus damage on crit
- **Effectiveness** - Land debuffs more reliably
- **Resistance** - Avoid enemy debuffs

## Gacha System

### Summon Rates

:::info[Standard Rates]

- ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Legendary: 1%
- ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Epic: 5%
- ⭐⭐⭐ Rare: 20%
- ⭐⭐ Common: 74%

:::

### Pity System

- **Soft pity** starts at 70 pulls
- **Hard pity** guarantees at 90 pulls
- Pity carries over between banners (usually)

## Energy System

Energy regenerates over time:
- **1 Energy** every **5 minutes**
- **Maximum cap** of 120-150 energy
- Overflow allowed from mail/rewards

:::tip[Efficiency Tip]

Use your energy before hitting the cap to avoid wasting natural regeneration!

:::
