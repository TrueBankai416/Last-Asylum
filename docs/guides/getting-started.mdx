# Getting Started with Last Asylum

Welcome to **Last Asylum**, survivor! This guide will help you navigate your first hours in the game and set you up for success.

## What is Last Asylum?

Last Asylum is a mobile strategy RPG where you build a team of survivors to fight through a post-apocalyptic world filled with dangers. Collect characters, upgrade your team, and survive the asylum!

## First Steps

### 1. Complete the Tutorial

The in-game tutorial will teach you the basics:
- How to navigate the interface
- Basic combat mechanics
- Character management
- Resource collection

:::tip[Pro Tip]

Pay attention during the tutorial! You'll receive valuable starter rewards for completing each step.

:::

### 2. Claim Your Starter Rewards

After completing the tutorial, make sure to:
- Check your mailbox for welcome gifts
- Claim daily login rewards
- Complete beginner missions for extra resources

### 3. Build Your First Team

Start with the characters you receive from the tutorial and early summons:
- Focus on leveling your main damage dealer first
- Keep a healer or support character in your team
- Don't spread resources too thin across many characters
