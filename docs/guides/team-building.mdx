# Team Building Guide

A well-built team is the foundation of success in Last Asylum. Learn how to create teams for every situation.

## Team Composition Basics

### The Core Roles

Every balanced team needs:

| Role | Purpose | Priority |
|------|---------|----------|
| **Tank** | Absorb damage, protect team | Essential |
| **DPS** | Deal damage, kill enemies | Essential |
| **Support** | Heal, buff, utility | High |
| **Control** | CC, debuffs, disrupt | Situational |

### Standard Compositions

**Balanced (Recommended for beginners):**
```
🛡️ Tank + ⚔️ DPS + ⚔️ DPS + 💚 Healer + 🎯 Flex
```

**Speed/Cleave (PvP):**
```
⚡ Speed Buffer + ⚔️ AoE DPS + ⚔️ AoE DPS + 🎯 Control + 🎯 Flex
```

**Sustain/Stall:**
```
🛡️ Tank + 🛡️ Tank + 💚 Healer + 💚 Healer + ⚔️ DPS
```

## Building for PvE

### Campaign Teams

Focus on:
- Strong single-target DPS for bosses
- AoE damage for wave clear
- At least one healer
- Synergistic elements/types

### Boss Fight Teams

```
Essential Elements:
├── Main DPS (your strongest damage dealer)
├── Defense Break (increases damage to boss)
├── Attack Buffer (boosts team damage)
├── Healer (sustain through fight)
└── Flex (shield, secondary DPS, or utility)
```

### Dungeon Specific

Each dungeon may require different strategies:
- **Fire Dungeon** → Bring Water characters
- **Water Dungeon** → Bring Nature characters
- **Nature Dungeon** → Bring Fire characters

## Building for PvP

### Arena Offense

Your goal: Kill before they kill you

**Key considerations:**
1. Speed - Go first or have immunity
2. Burst damage - One-shot potential
3. Strip/Control - Remove enemy buffs

### Arena Defense

Your goal: Make enemies skip your team

**Deterrent strategies:**
- High threat + high speed
- Tanky sustain that takes forever
- RNG-based (counter, dodge)

## Synergy Examples

### Attack Team
When your DPS gets an attack buff AND defense breaks the enemy:
```
Damage = (Base × 1.5 attack buff) vs (Enemy DEF × 0.3 def break)
       = Massive damage increase!
```

### Control Chain
```
Unit A: Stuns enemy
Unit B: Extends stun duration  
Unit C: Adds Silence
Result: Enemy never gets a turn
```

## Team Building Checklist

- [ ] Do I have a reliable way to survive? (tank/healer)
- [ ] Do I have enough damage to clear in time?
- [ ] Do I have answers to enemy threats?
- [ ] Are my speed stats tuned correctly?
- [ ] Does my team have synergy?

## Common Mistakes

:::warning[Avoid These:]

- All DPS, no sustain → Die to chip damage
- All tanks, no damage → Time out on bosses
- Ignoring speed → Enemy goes first, you lose
- No cleanse/immunity → Debuffed to death

:::
